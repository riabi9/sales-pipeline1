ℹ️ Formula Notes

Sheet/Plate: W = Thickness(m) × Width(m) × Length(m) × 7850

Round Tube: W = π/4 × [(OD² − ID²)] × Length × 7850

Square/Rect Tube: W = [(a × b) − (a−2t)(b−2t)] × Length × 7850

Round Bar: W = π/4 × D² × Length × 7850

Flat Bar: W = Width(m) × Thickness(m) × Length × 7850

Default density 7850 kg/m³ (steel). Adjust for stainless (7900-8000) or alloys.
